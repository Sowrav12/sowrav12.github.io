Sazzad Steel Corporation is a business where all kinds of steel materials are sold for building a structure, such as houses and other buildings. In this business, they sell angle bars, square bars, diamond plates, profile sheets, box pipe, and much more. Sazzad Steel Corporation is located in Chandina, Bangladesh and Md Sazzad Hossain has been running this business since 2007 and has dedicated his life to make this a growing business.
